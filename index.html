About Pincraft

Quietly building the implements
inside the world's
best beauty brands.

Founded in 1998 in Sonepat, India, Pincraft Industries has spent more than two decades perfecting the craft of beauty implements. What started with a small range of professional tweezers has grown into a complete portfolio of cuticle nippers, blackhead removers, gua sha tools and allied products for the cosmetic industry.

As design experts, we innovate with new ranges and seasonal collections — partnering with brands on bespoke designs and packaging. Every piece carries the same standard of finish whether it ships in a luxury retail box or a salon's bulk order.
